Coherence is a fundamental concept in wireless communications, referring to the regions in time, frequency, and space over which the wireless channel remains approximately constant. These regions are quantified by coherence time, coherence bandwidth, and spatial (or angular) coherence.

The DFT is not just a mathematical tool but also a means of exploring signal behavior in each of these domains. In wireless communications, the DFT enables resolution of signal components in frequency (via OFDM), in time (via delay profiling), and in angle (via beamforming).
